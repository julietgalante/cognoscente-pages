Cognoscente Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Cognoscente helps users analyze articles, essays, and newsletters, track their reactions, and discover patterns in their reading over time.

Information stored on your device

Cognoscente stores your reading history, article titles, URLs, sources, estimated reading time, topics, key points, reactions, skipped status, bookmarks, and reading patterns locally on your device.

Information sent for analysis

To generate article analysis and reading patterns, Cognoscente sends article title, URL or domain, extracted article text, and saved article metadata to its analysis service.

Use of AI services

Cognoscente uses third-party AI infrastructure to generate article analysis and reading patterns.

No sale of reading data

Cognoscente does not sell your reading data. Cognoscente does not use your reading data for advertising tracking.

Deleting data

You can delete locally stored Cognoscente data by deleting the app from your device.
